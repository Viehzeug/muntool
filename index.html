ETH MUN

ETH Model United Nations was founded in 2007 by two enthusiastic students who wanted to see the principle of Model United Nations implemented at Europe’s leading technological university.

Ever since that moment Members of ETH MUN have organized weekly meetings to simulate the work and the intense debates of the UN and to prepare for international MUN conferences where they would debate with other committed students from all over the world and win awards for their outstanding diplomatic and negotiation skills.

Today, ETH MUN is one of the biggest commissions of the VSETH (Students' Union of the ETH) with an ever growing number of members and an active Alumni network.
